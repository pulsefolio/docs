## Common Scam Tactics

Be aware of these common scam tactics:

- **Fake websites** with similar URLs (e.g., pulsefolio.com instead of pulsefolio.io)
- **Impersonation** on social media with slight variations in usernames
- **Unsolicited DMs** claiming to be from Pulsefolio team members
- **Fake support** offering to "help" with your wallet or account
- **Phishing emails** with malicious links

## What to Do If You're Targeted

<Steps>
  <Step title="Don't Panic">
    Stay calm and don't rush into any actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Source">
    Check if the communication is from an official Pulsefolio channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Never Share Private Keys">
    Never share your private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords with anyone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report the Incident">
    Report suspicious activity to the official Pulsefolio channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>
