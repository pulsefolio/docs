## Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Application">
    Projects submit an application with details about their token, LP, or NFT collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Our team reviews the project for safety, quality, and compliance with PulseChain standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integration">
    Approved projects are integrated into Pulsefolio's tracking and display systems.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visibility">
    Projects become visible in portfolios, activity feeds, and discovery sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Onboarded

- **Tokens** - Native project tokens and utility tokens
- **Liquidity Pools** - PulseX LP tokens and farming opportunities
- **NFT Collections** - Digital collectibles and project-specific NFTs
- **Staking Contracts** - Project staking and reward mechanisms

<Info>
  The onboarding process ensures that only legitimate, safe, and valuable projects are displayed on Pulsefolio, protecting users while giving quality projects meaningful exposure.
</Info>

<Note>
  Project onboarding is ongoing as the PulseChain ecosystem grows, ensuring Pulsefolio always reflects the best and most current projects in the space.
</Note>
