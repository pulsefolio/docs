Pulsefolio includes a built-in swap feature powered by a DEX aggregator. This allows users to trade tokens directly inside the dashboard while automatically routing through the best available rates across PulseChain.

## Non-Custodial Trading

Swapping is fully non-custodial — you always trade directly from your connected wallet. Pulsefolio simply provides the interface and aggregator integration to make the process faster and easier.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Connect your wallet to access the swap feature within Pulsefolio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Tokens">
    Choose the tokens you want to swap from and to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rate Optimization">
    Pulsefolio automatically finds the best rates across all PulseChain DEXs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute Trade">
    Confirm and execute the trade directly from your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

With Swap, Pulsefolio becomes more than just a place to track your assets — it's also the easiest way to take action when you see opportunities.

<Info>
  All swap transactions are automatically tracked in your Recent Activity feed and reflected in your portfolio balances.
</Info>

<Note>
  The swap feature maintains the same non-custodial principles as the rest of Pulsefolio - your keys, your tokens, your control.
</Note>
