## Activity Types

Pulsefolio captures all major on-chain activities:

- **Token Transfers** - Incoming and outgoing transfers of all supported tokens
- **NFT Transactions** - Purchases, sales, and transfers of digital collectibles
- **Liquidity Operations** - Adding, removing, and managing PulseX liquidity positions
- **Staking Actions** - Staking, unstaking, and claiming rewards
- **Contract Interactions** - Smart contract calls and interactions

<Info>
  All activities are automatically detected and added to your feed in real-time as they occur on-chain.
</Info>
