## Growing Ecosystem

As new NFT projects complete the onboarding process, they will be added to Pulsefolio for full tracking and display. This ensures that every collection shown is properly supported, giving collectors confidence and projects visibility within the PulseChain ecosystem.

<Info>
  Only verified NFT projects that complete our onboarding process are displayed on Pulsefolio, ensuring quality and proper support for all collections.
</Info>

## Integration with Portfolio

Pulsefolio makes managing your digital collectibles simple, smart, and fully aligned with the rest of your portfolio. Your NFTs are seamlessly integrated with your token holdings, LP positions, and staking rewards for a complete view of your PulseChain assets.

<Note>
  Your NFT collection value is included in your overall portfolio calculations and PnL tracking.
</Note>
