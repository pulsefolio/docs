## Features Overview

- **Public Profiles** - Customizable profiles with name, avatar, and bio
- **Social Interactions** - Follow, connect, and interact with other users
- **Content Sharing** - Share updates, achievements, and portfolio highlights
- **Privacy Controls** - Full control over your visibility and interactions
- **Community Discovery** - Find and connect with other PulseChain enthusiasts

<Info>
  All social features are completely optional. You can use Pulsefolio purely as a portfolio tracker or engage with the community as much as you'd like.
</Info>

<Note>
  Community features are designed to enhance your PulseChain experience while maintaining your privacy and control over your information.
</Note>
